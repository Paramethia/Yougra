How to get an album link?

Mobile

Go to the album you wanna download on YouTube music, tap the share icon, and choose to copy. There you go. Now you have the link.

Computer

While scrolling looking at a list of albums on YouTube music on PC, just click those 3 dots and you should see an option to share and copy the link.

Download method

Immediate

This ensures that every video you download always start immediatly without processing. Keep in mind that the file may show up as corrupted for some devices.

Process

This ensures that the video works on any device and is also seekable by having a process of downloading and merging on the server, then the download will start. Not recommended for large downloads if you are inpatient as it will take a long time before it starts the download. There is also a max file size requirement (5GB) for downloading videos for this option.

What is Yougra about?

Yougra is a web app that allows you to download videos, music videos, songs, and more. Simply search for what you want above and click the video you want to download as either video or audio. Once you select a video, you will be able to choose from different video quality options before downloading.

Help

In addition to searching, you can paste the exact YouTube video or song link in the "Use link" section located above the search bar on the right. In this section, you can also paste YouTube playlist or album links. Playlists and albums are currently downloadable as audio only. If you're unsure how to get a playlist link, you can find instructions in the Settings menu at the top right (the gear icon).

Speaking of Settings, you will also find download methods there for videos. There are two methods: Immediate and Process. By default, the Process method is selected, but you can switch between them at any time. The differences between these methods are explained in the Settings menu.
